Support — Isaac's World

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Isaac's World is a private, invite-only app for a family to record, share, and watch videos together.

Getting started

Children can record, decorate, and publish videos to the family's private channel. A parent manages invitations, moderation, and other parent settings from the app's PIN-protected parent controls.

Common questions

  • Who can see videos? Only family members specifically invited through the app's private sharing flow can view them.
  • How do I join a family channel? Ask the parent who manages the channel to send you an invitation.
  • Why can't I change parent settings? Parent controls are protected with a PIN.
  • What should I do if I have trouble with an invitation? Contact the parent or family member who invited you.

Contact
