Privacy Policy — Isaac's World

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Isaac's World is a private, invite-only app for a single family to record, share, and watch each other's videos. It is designed for use by children under the supervision of a parent or guardian. This policy explains how the app handles information.

The short version

We do not collect, sell, or transmit your personal information to the app's developer or to any third party. There are no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind. All content stays within your family's own private Apple iCloud.

What the app stores, and where

Everything you create in the app is stored in one of two places, both under your control:

  • On your device: videos you are recording or editing, and small preferences such as your chosen display name, channel name, and channel emoji.
  • In your own Apple iCloud (via Apple's CloudKit): videos you publish, together with the reactions, comments, and display names of family members you have invited. This data lives in your iCloud account and shared iCloud space. The developer of this app has no access to it and cannot read it.

Camera and microphone

The app uses the camera and microphone only to record videos when you choose to. Recordings are never streamed or uploaded anywhere except your own iCloud when you deliberately publish them.

Children's privacy

This app is intended for family use with children. It does not collect personal information from children, does not contain advertising, does not include in-app purchases, and does not link out to the web or social media. A parent or guardian controls who is invited and approves videos before they are shared within the family.

Data sharing and deletion

Content is shared only with the specific family members a parent invites through Apple's private sharing. You can delete any video from within the app. Because all data is held in your own Apple iCloud, you can also remove it at any time through your Apple ID / iCloud settings.
